What is BetBuddys?

BetBuddys is a social app where friends create private groups and place bets using virtual currency only. Everyone starts with $1,000 in fake money. No real money is involved — ever.

FAQ

Is real money involved?
No. BetBuddys uses virtual currency only. You cannot deposit, withdraw, or exchange any real money.

How do I delete my account?
Go to Profile tab → scroll to the bottom → tap "Delete Account". This permanently removes your account and all associated data.

How do I join a group?
Ask a friend for their 6-character invite code, then tap "Join Group" and enter the code.

I didn't receive my magic link email.
Check your spam folder. If it's not there, try again after a few minutes. Make sure you entered the correct email address.
